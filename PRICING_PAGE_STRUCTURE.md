### Buttons
- **Primary CTA:** Rose-500 background, white text

- **Color:** Rose-600
- **Hover:** Rose-700
- **Underline:** On hover

### Expandable FAQ
- **Closed:** Chevron pointing down
- **Open:** Chevron rotated 180°
- **Animation:** Smooth rotate transition
