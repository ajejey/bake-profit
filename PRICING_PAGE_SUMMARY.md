## 📊 Pricing Model

### Free Plan
- **Price:** $0/forever
- **Recipes:** 5
- **Orders/month:** 15

- **Inventory:** 20 items
- **Analytics:** Basic

- **Customers:** Unlimited
- **Inventory:** Unlimited
- **Analytics:** Advanced
- **Storage:** Local + Google Drive sync
- **Support:** Priority
- **Branding:** No BakeProfit branding
